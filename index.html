About

IEEE MACE SB

IEEE Student Branch MACE (Student Branch Code: 32041) was established on November 17th, 1988. Being one of the largest student branches across the globe with over 500 student members, diverse events are organized to heighten the skills of our students within diversified domains and equip them with the rapidly changing industry. A great deal of pride goes to the Student Branch's volunteers, who actively take part at various levels, are extremely active, and are always eager to take on new challenges. It is matter of utmost satisfaction to organize multiple events to meet the needs of students in their respective areas of interest, molding them into better professionals who will in turn shape a better tomorrow..
